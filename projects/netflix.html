Netflix Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Tools: Python • Pandas • Matplotlib • Seaborn

Project Overview

The goal of this analysis was to uncover patterns in Netflix content such as genre popularity, content growth over time, country contributions, and audience rating distributions.

Dataset

Dataset: Netflix Movies and TV Shows from Kaggle (8,807 records). It includes title, director, cast, country, rating, listed genres, and release year.

Key Questions

  • How has Netflix’s content library grown over the years?
  • Which genres are the most common?
  • Which countries contribute the most content?
  • How are titles rated (TV-MA, TV-14, etc.)?

Key Findings

  • Netflix has seen a major increase in TV Show releases since 2016.
  • Drama and Comedy are the most dominant genres.
  • The majority of content is rated TV-MA and TV-14.
  • The United States and India contribute the largest share of Netflix titles.
